Experiment 01: Text Size Sweep

Tests whether requested text size changes success/failure across English, Arabic, and Chinese.

Observed Result

English stays correct at large, medium, and small sizes. Arabic is malformed even at large size, so this run does not support a simple low-resolution-only explanation. Chinese remains much stronger than Arabic under the same prompt template.
